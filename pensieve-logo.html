Design notes

The signature: rather than drawing a separate book and a separate basin, the open book's pages curve into the basin shape itself — so the object that holds her memories of reading and the object that holds magical memories are literally the same line.

Palette: midnight indigo and gilded gold read as "magical" without copying anything Warner Bros. owns — no specific house colors, no canon symbols. Lavender/silver mist nods to the Pensieve's memory-strands without quoting them exactly.

Type: Cormorant Garamond for the wordmark — elegant, a little old-world, readable at small sizes for an app icon label.
